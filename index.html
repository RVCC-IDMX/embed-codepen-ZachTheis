Pen: Candy Color Button Animation

by Yuhomyan

This pen is an example of some neat neuomorphic hover effects you can achieve with css. Each button has a different animation that triggers when you hover over it with the mouse. The buttons don't do anything when clicked - although they could be made to do so - this is just to showcase the animated effects.

See the Pen Candy Color Button Animation by Yuhomyan (@yuhomyan) on CodePen.
